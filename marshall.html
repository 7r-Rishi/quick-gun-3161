From the stage to your living room, Marshall brings you the powerful sound of Rock n Roll. This amplifier and headphone major has spent countless trips with the best musicians and performers to refine their products so that you get the best experience possible. Experience the Marshall heritage just like your favourite artists already have. Marshall has been celebrated by some of the greatest bands and musicians like Jimi Hendrix, Eric Clapton, Slash, Oasis, Muse, Gorillaz and Bring Me The Horizon.


Marshall initially started as a humble music store known as Jim Marshall and Son in Hanwell, London. With the demand for new kinds of amplifiers increasing, the 'Number One' (first of the numerous JTM45 amps) was introduced and the rest, as they say, is history. Of course, why settle only on amps when you can break barriers elsewhere? In 2010, Marshall entered the headphone market with the Major and Minor lines. Marshal over the years has built a strong presence globally and operates through their Marshall India website domestically.
